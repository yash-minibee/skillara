Our Journey

From a Garage to Global Impact

In 2019, our founders — a UX designer and a digital marketer — were frustrated. They'd spent thousands on courses that were outdated, theoretical, and disconnected from what employers actually wanted.

So they built EduPro: a platform where every course is designed backwards from job requirements. Real projects. Real mentors. Real outcomes.

Today, EduPro serves 50,000+ students across 80+ countries, with graduates working at companies like Google, Shopify, HubSpot, and hundreds of fast-growing startups.
